About Me

Researcher. Educator. Leader.

I am a PhD researcher at the Institute of Environmental Science, University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh, focusing on environmental dynamics in the Sundarbans Mangrove Forest — one of the world's most ecologically critical regions.

My research uses multi-temporal Landsat imagery spanning 1975–2025 and MODIS NDVI time series to detect land use and land cover changes across five decades, applying Maximum Likelihood Classification and GIS-based spatial analysis to understand how this unique ecosystem is transforming under environmental and anthropogenic pressures.

Beyond research, I serve as President of the Rajshahi University Music Club, and I am increasingly engaged at the intersection of science, business, and technology — believing that research insights should translate into real-world impact.

Research Focus

What I Study

My research sits at the intersection of environmental science, spatial analysis, and remote sensing technology — applied to one of South Asia's most vital ecosystems.

🌳

Mangrove Cover Change

Decadal analysis of mangrove forest dynamics in the Bangladeshi Sundarbans using Landsat imagery from 1975 to 2025 — tracking gain, loss, and transition patterns.

🗺

LULC Classification

Applying Maximum Likelihood Classification and post-classification comparison methods to multi-temporal satellite data for accurate land cover mapping and change detection.

📈

NDVI Time Series

MODIS NDVI time series analysis (2001–2025) to track vegetation health, seasonal patterns, and long-term trends in the Sundarbans Biosphere Reserve.

🌊

River & Coastal Dynamics

Monitoring riverbank erosion, accretion, and coastal morphological changes using NDWI and MNDWI indices derived from multi-sensor satellite data.

📡

Google Earth Engine

Cloud-based geospatial analysis using GEE for large-scale data processing, time series computation, and automated change detection workflows.

🔬

Accuracy Assessment

Rigorous classification accuracy evaluation using error matrices, kappa coefficients, and Intensity Analysis to validate remote sensing outputs.
